Conveniently Located

Security for your Possessions

Beyond the standard padlock, 390 Self Storage offers additional security for your possessions. The entire facility is fenced with barbed wire where more easily accessible. The security gate is controlled by your unique personal access code. It is well-lit and constantly monitored by camera. The entire facility is well-lit with state of the art security cameras that record 24 hours a day. The climate controlled storage requires your access code to enter and has a fire protection sprinkler system.

For Your Convenience

Convenience starts with drive-up accessibility, 24-7. Our online portal offers 24-hour account management where payment can be made. For personal assistance, the on-site office management is always happy to help.
