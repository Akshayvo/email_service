Affordable Self Storage Units & Recreational Vehicle Parking in Westerly

Friendship Self Storage is Westerly’s one stop self storage solution. Our wide selection of unit sizes and vehicle storage spaces are all available at extremely affordable rates. As a locally owned and operated business, we work hard every day to bring you the kind of customer service and quality storage options that set us apart from the big chains. Take a moment to check out our rates, then reserve your unit, or call our friendly management team today!

Convenience

We built Friendship Self Storage from the ground up with convenience in mind. Our single-level facility is conveniently located on Friendship Street between West Street and Marriott Ave on Pleasant Street, making our facility very easy to pull in and out of, even if you’re driving one of our moving trucks. You can check out our inventory online, then reserve your unit in seconds with our easy tenant portal, or you can give our office a call, and one of our friendly staff will be happy to perform a contactless rental. Once you’re a tenant, you’ll have access to your unit(s) during our extended access hours from 6:00AM to 9:00PM 7 days a week!

Security

The team at Friendship Self Storage knows there’s more to picking out a storage unit than just the conveniences. That’s why we go the extra mile to create an environment that feels safe and secure at all times. Our well-lit self storage facility is covered by security cameras, and access is controlled through a pin-pad activated electronic gate.
